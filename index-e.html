Coronation Hall

Traditional West Quebec Style Ciders and Heritage Foods, by the Graham Family of Bristol.

Coronation Hall was built in the early 1930s as the MacDougall Dance Hall, but renamed for the coronation of George VI in 1937.

We rescued this iconic building in 2003, and in 2008 opened the first commercial cidery in the region. Come visit us today! For the latest events news, please connect with us on our Facebook page.

We welcome bus tours and groups.
