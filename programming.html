Daily Programs

Tour the Cider Mill
Tours of our cider mill are available any time we’re open, but a phone call or email to tell us you’re coming is always appreciated (so that we’ll know if we’re pressing that day). Take some extra time and visit our 1930′s era dance hall with many unique antiques and oddities.

Tastings
Any time we’re open, we’re open for tastings of our sweet and hard ciders. You must be eighteen years or older to have alcohol in Quebec (sorry!).

Orchard Visit
We have an ‘orchard visit’ program suitable for elementary school aged children. Through games and exploration we teach the children about the orchard, where apples come from, farm life, and farm animals. The program can be tailored; please contact us for more information.

‘U-Pick’ is not offered yet, but should be available in the next few years.

High Tea
We also have a popular program with retirement homes, seniors' groups and bus tours. Enjoy a high tea (lunch and dessert) in our tea room, try our alcoholic and non-alcoholic ciders, see how they are made, and tour our mill and dance hall. We are wheelchair accessible and special nutritional needs can be accomodated. Please call to find out more and reserve your visit.
